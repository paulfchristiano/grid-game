  • Use arrow keys to select a path from the left to right of the grid.
  • The path must go straight through each square, and turn 90 degrees at each diamond.
  • For each number, the path must traverse exactly that many of the adjacent edges.
  • For each cycle, the path must traverse an adjacent edge in the indicated orientation.
  • A symbol will block illegal moves, and turn black once it is satisfied.
  • The goal on the right side will turn black once all symbols are satisfied.
  • After finishing a level, press enter or space to move onto the next level.
  • You can undo a move by pressing 'z' or retracing your steps.
  • You can restart the level by pressing space. This leaves the old path as a grey shadow.
  • You can retrace your old steps by pressing 'x' when you are on the grey shadow.
  • This is a helpful way to modify the beginning of a trajectory.
  • Your personal best score is computed as the bottom of a confidence interval.
